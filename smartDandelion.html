Smart Dandelion :

Smart mobile flight installations provide shelter to citizens

Lackness of Infrastructure in Crowded Chinese Cities

With the rapid development of urbanization, citizens complained that it is hard to find a comfortable place to relax in the crowded streets,because of the variety of population and the city’s fascinating expansion speed.

Concept

By using specific breeding strategies, some plants are able to respond quickly to the changes in environment, as well as the rapid changes in ecosystems. We hope that, by imitating specific physiological properties of plants, we can design a kind of urban infrastructure device to provide people with decent and flexible service, so that the urban space can be effectively utilized.

Strategy

These devices will drift above the city as dandelions, and follow citizens’ demands as the plant follow the direction of wind. Then they will take root into the city, breed and migrate. At the same time, these devices can adjust to the sun and users flexibly, providing people with more appropriate services.

Bionic Strategy

Mimic plants organ's bionic principles on installation to realize smart reaction to the change of the environment and efficient function to generate energy.

Adaptability

Smart dandelion devices make flexible adjustments to the change of light environment and users’ needs. On one hand, by the bionic imitation of plant’s phototropism, the solar panel of the smart dandelion can follow the sun flexibly, providing people with better sunshade and improve the utilization rate of solar energy.

Flexibility

On the other hand, by adjusting the seats, panels, and organizing mode, smart dandelions can meet the needs of various types of activities of all types of people.
