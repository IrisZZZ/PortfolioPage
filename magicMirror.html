Magic Mirror :

Interactive Installation helps to understand everyone's self indentity

Discription

Magic Mirror has been designed to create an environment which allows people to immerse themselves into concept of truly seeing their internal self. This interactive project represents the intersections of a diverse campus population identities and lived experiences through the mirror of the vanity. Based on the concepts and themes used in the exhibition, this piece is designed to create a space for the participant to think about their true identity and emotions. It gives the visitors a chance of connecting to the emotions of the story tellers and helps them in seeing themselves in the struggles of others, by creating a moment of honesty and self-reflection.

Materials

Motion Sensors, Raspberry Pie, Raley, Wires, Cameras
Monitors, Spearkers, light
Processing, After Effect

Interaction

The participant sits in front of the mirror, which is an interactive screen with a transparent mirror on top that lights up when a person is present and sitting on the chair. By gazing in the mirror, the participants see their face while written stories along with recordings play the oral histories. These stories are community members' responses to questions about who they are, their internal self, and how they see themselves in the mirror.
